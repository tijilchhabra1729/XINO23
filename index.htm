Who are we?

XINO comprises of numerous technology enthusiasts with a fiery passion for computer science. Each member has a unique perspective and a range of skills and the urge to innovate. The club members value the different ideas and aspirations of each other and work together to realize and achieve them. Ever since its inception, the members of the club have won innumerable events but never let the glory get in the way of learning. The members appreciate their achievements but never stop their crusade for more and continue on a quest to learn and reach greater heights.
